Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recruitment services and processes.

How long does the recruitment process typically take?

The timeline varies based on role complexity and client requirements. For most positions, we can present qualified candidates within 5-7 business days, with the entire process typically completed in 2-4 weeks.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we serve multiple sectors, we have particular expertise in IT, BFSI, Healthcare, Engineering, and Manufacturing. Our consultants have deep industry knowledge in these areas.

What are your fees for job seekers?

Our services are completely free for candidates. We're compensated by our client companies when we successfully place candidates in their organizations.

How do you ensure candidate quality?

All candidates undergo a rigorous 5-step screening process including resume verification, technical assessments, reference checks, and in-person interviews with our consultants.

Do you offer temporary staffing solutions?

Yes, we provide flexible contract staffing options for projects, seasonal needs, and interim positions. Our temp-to-perm options also allow clients to evaluate candidates before making permanent offers.

What makes your consultancy different?

Our industry specialization, personalized approach, and 95% placement success rate set us apart. We focus on long-term career matches rather than just filling positions.
