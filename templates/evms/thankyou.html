Our Review Process

Here's what happens next with your application:

1-2 Business Days

Initial Screening

Our recruitment team will review your qualifications and experience to ensure they match our requirements. This includes verification of your documents and work history.

3-5 Business Days

Assessment Phase

Depending on the position, you may receive skills assessment tests via email. These help us better understand your capabilities.

Within 1 Week

Interview Scheduling

If selected, our coordinator will contact you to schedule an interview at your convenience. Interviews may be conducted in-person or via video call.

7-10 Business Days

Final Decision

You'll receive notification of our hiring decision. Successful candidates will get an offer package with all joining details.

How to Prepare

While we review your application, here's how you can prepare for the next steps:

Stay Accessible

Keep your phone and email active. We may contact you for additional information or to schedule interviews.

Review Job Requirements

Revisit the job description and think of examples that demonstrate how your skills match the requirements.

Prepare for Virtual Meetings

Ensure you have a quiet space and reliable internet connection for potential video interviews.

Gather Documents

Have digital copies of your certificates, ID proofs, and other relevant documents ready.
